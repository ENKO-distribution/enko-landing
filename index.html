Paid campaigns on Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube, a promo plan for every release, and influencer campaigns end to end. It’s the reason the numbers on this page look the way they do.

Eurovision. Twice.

The biggest music event in Europe, watched by 160M+ people a year. Kalush Orchestra won it in 2022 with “Stefania.” alyona alyona & Jerry Heil went top-3 in 2024 with “Teresa & Maria.” Both were full-management ENKO artists, with our team behind everything from release to stage.

Platform programs

Our artists front the platforms’ own campaigns: Apple Music Sessions, Apple Music Up Next, and Spotify EQUAL. Banners, covers, playlists, special sessions, and billboards over Times Square. Eight ENKO artists have fronted Spotify’s EQUAL program alone.
